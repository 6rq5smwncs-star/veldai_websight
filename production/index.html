FAQ

Common questions.

Which areas do you serve?

We work with healthcare practices across all of Zimbabwe and South Africa — from Harare, Bulawayo and Victoria Falls to Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban and Pretoria. Everything is delivered remotely, so your location is never a barrier.

What is AEO, and why does my practice need it?

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization — makes your practice the answer when patients ask AI tools like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overviews, Perplexity or Gemini for a clinic. More people now ask an assistant instead of scrolling Google. We structure your website and data so those engines understand, trust and recommend you by name.

Do you only do automation, or full websites too?

Both. We design and build the website itself, handle SEO and AEO so patients can find it, and layer automation on top so enquiries become kept appointments. You can start with any one piece — most practices begin with the audit.

Do we need to replace our existing practice software?

No. Our systems integrate with what you already use — no disruption to your team or your workflow. Whether you run a bespoke practice management system or something simpler, we work around it, not over it.

How long does setup take?

A new website typically takes two to three weeks; automation runs in parallel and is usually live within two. The audit and scoping take three to five business days, and your team is barely involved until launch day.

What if something breaks or stops working?

We monitor everything continuously. Your retainer includes full support and we fix issues before you notice them. You're not managing the technology — that's our job, and we take it seriously.
